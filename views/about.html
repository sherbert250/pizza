Us

Phoenix House of Pizza & Subs first opened its doors on April 23, 1992. Since then, our main priority has always been to serve Fast and Well. We do not only want to be a good pizzeria, but a great one!And what would a great pizzeria be without the best product? We make our own dough daily, on the premises, in small batches to ensure freshness. Our sauce is our own secret family recipe. We've perfected our blend of spices. And, what would a pizza be without the right cheese? We have chosen a cheese that achieves an amazing texture and flavor. Therefore, in each bite you take, you will taste a pizza unlike anything you never had before.
